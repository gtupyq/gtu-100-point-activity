About This GTU Student Opportunity Board

This site was built to save GTU engineering students time. Instead of searching multiple platforms for a GTU free certificate or a quiz that counts toward GTU 100 Point Activity, everything is checked and listed here in one place — updated roughly every two weeks so you don't waste time on expired links.

Every GTU activity listed here links out to the original platform (such as Unstop and similar trusted sites) where you actually take part. Always keep your certificate as proof, and confirm point values with your college's activity-point coordinator, since GTU's official point table can change year to year.
