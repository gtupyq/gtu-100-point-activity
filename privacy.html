Privacy Policy

This page explains, in plain language, how GTU 100 Point Activity handles information.

What we collect

This site does not require sign-up or login. We don't collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number unless you choose to submit it through the Contact page.

Affiliate links

Some "Participate Now" buttons and activity images link to third-party platforms through affiliate links. If you sign up or take part through these links, the third-party platform may pay us a small commission — at no extra cost to you. This does not affect which activities are listed or how they're described.

Third-party platforms

Clicking through to a quiz, hackathon, internship or webinar takes you to a third-party website. Their own privacy policy applies once you leave this site — we don't control or see your data on those platforms.

Cookies & analytics

This site may use basic, privacy-friendly analytics to understand which activities are useful to visitors. No personal data is sold to third parties.

Email subscriptions

If you choose to subscribe using the "Subscribe for Free Updates" form, we collect your email address in order to send you updates about new GTU certificates, quizzes and opportunities. We use Brevo (formerly Sendinblue), a third-party email service provider, to manage this list and send these emails. Brevo processes your email address on our behalf according to its own privacy policy.

After subscribing, you may receive an automated welcome email confirming your subscription. Every newsletter or update email we send includes a one-click unsubscribe link in the footer, so you can stop receiving emails at any time without contacting us. We do not sell or share your email address with anyone other than Brevo, solely for the purpose of sending you these emails.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the Contact page.
